About Me

Hello! I'm Olanrewaju Olamide, a software developer with 3.5+ years of experience in backend and fullstack development. I specialize in building robust APIs, SaaS platforms, and custom management systems, with growing expertise in DevOps for efficient deployment and scaling.

I’m the founder of PrepStack, an application that provides students access to verified past questions for download, and Billently, an invoice generator app designed to help freelancers and small business owners manage their finances with ease and professionalism.

As a self-taught developer, I’ve built and deployed projects across multiple industries, including education, fintech, and business automation, with a strong focus on building scalable and secure backend systems. Beyond code, I’m passionate about learning, mentoring, and simplifying complex ideas through intuitive digital solutions that make life and business smoother for everyone.
